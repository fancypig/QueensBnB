Frequently Asked Questions

Your First Trip

At first it might seem a little intimidating but just think of it as staying at a hotel or inn. Our hosts are always welcoming and you can read the reviwes left by previous customers to assure this. Make sure to pack all the necessities as they are not provided on site and have fun!

Common Questions

When Do I Pay

You pay when you book a room however the payment is withheld from the host until you arrive at your destintion. So if you're worried about cancelling a booking and not getting your money back. We've got you covered.

Is It Safe

Nothing is 100% risk free. However, we gaurantee that your Queen's Alumni are great people and can be trusted to give you a great experience!

Hosting

The process is very clear and simple. Take a picture of your place, choose a price provide some back up info and your listing is completed! If you want to host your place click the link in the navigation bar at the top of the screen!
